About Studently

Studently is an all-in-one platform designed to help students achieve academic success with ease and efficiency. Our app seamlessly integrates with Google Classroom to automatically fetch and sync assignment data, deadlines, and class materials, ensuring that students never miss a task.

With Studently, students can stay organized, focused, and on top of their academic goals by accessing a suite of powerful tools tailored to their needs.

Features

  • Google Classroom Integration: Seamlessly fetch and sync assignments, deadlines, and materials.
  • Intuitive Calendar: Manage assignments, deadlines, and exam dates effortlessly.
  • GPA Calculator: Track academic performance and progress.
  • Personalized Study Tools: Get tailored study guides, flashcards, and practice tests based on your coursework.
  • Comprehensive To-Do List: Stay organized with real-time updates ensuring no task is missed.
