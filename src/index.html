Studently is an all-in-one platform designed to help students achieve academic success with ease and efficiency. Our app seamlessly integrates with Google Classroom to automatically fetch and sync assignment data, deadlines, and class materials, ensuring that students never miss a task. Key features include an intuitive calendar to manage assignments, deadlines, and exams, a GPA calculator to track academic performance, and a comprehensive to-do list to keep tasks organized. Additionally, Studently includes a powerful study tool that generates personalized study guides, flashcards, and practice tests based on the student's coursework. With Studently, students can stay organized, focused, and on top of their academic goals, all while benefiting from real-time updates pulled directly from Google Classroom.
